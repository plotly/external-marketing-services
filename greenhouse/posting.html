Senior Business Analyst

at Plotly (View all jobs)
Montreal

Plotly Technologies is a Montreal-based software company leading the way in data visualization and analytics apps for the enterprise building off their strength in the open-source software world. Plotly makes it possible for high-value Data Science teams to deliver on and operationalize AI / ML, analytics and digital reporting initiatives with Dash Enterprise.

Job Summary:  We are currently looking for a Senior Business Analyst to contribute to the structuring of the growing operations and finance teams in Montreal. The successful candidate will have an influential role in driving the company’s growth by implementing report automation, benchmarking performance, and optimizing operational processes. You will actively assist the VP Finance and work to find solutions to complex business challenges to provide insight and recommendations to key stakeholders across all functional area.

 

Job Responsibilities

  • Develop, automate and maintain custom reports and dashboards;
  • Determine and monitor relevant KPIs to the leadership team in a timely and consistent manner (weekly, monthly and quarterly);
  • Interpret data and analyze results to continuously uncover areas of improvement;
  • Support ongoing understanding of business results against objectives;
  • Escalate issues encountered related to data/reports as soon as they become apparent while providing viable solutions and options to address these challenges;
  • Identify, analyze, and interpret trends from data sets pulled from Salesforce, Pardot, HR and financial systems;
  • Identify and recommend opportunities to improve productivity, effectiveness and operational efficiency;
  • Develop and leverage data collection techniques, data analytics, and other strategies that verify actual data and statistical accuracy and quality.
  • Deliver ad hoc projects as required;
  • Participate in continuous improvements and internal controls;

Required Skills

Knowledge and Experience

  • Three to five years of experience as a Business Analyst
  • Strong analytics and IT systems skills
  • Bachelor's degree in analytics, management, a related field or equivalent experience in financial planning and analysis;
  • Advanced knowledge of CRM and analytics systems 
  • Excellent project management skills: collecting requirements, drafting of specifications, follow-up, testing;

Skills

  • Proven working experience as a data analyst with strong analytical skills with the ability to collect, organize, analyze, and disseminate significant amounts of information with strong attention to detail and accuracy.
  • Technical expertise regarding data models, database design development, data mining, and segmentation techniques.
  • Agility and open-mindedness, to adapt to the constraints and challenges of an ever-changing environment.
  • Excellent ability to plan, develop and coordinate different projects concurrently.
  • Curiosity, synthetic mind and ability to articulate abstract concepts.
  • Autonomy, professionalism and results-oriented.
  • Ability to set priorities and good tolerance for ambiguity.
  • Excellent communication and listening skills.
  • A team spirit with excellent interpersonal and communication skills is essential to dealing with diverse resources.

Assets

  • Mastery of Salesforce and Pardot an asset
  • Past experience as a developer or technical training (an asset);
  • Knowledge of Python (an asset)
