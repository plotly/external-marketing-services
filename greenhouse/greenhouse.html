Plotly

Plotly is a data visualization company focused on making it easy to deliver interactive apps, charts and graphics that enable exploration and sharing of insights and ideas. Plotly’s products, Dash and Chart Studio, along with its Graphing Libraries, make it easy to create beautiful, interactive data visualizations and charts - in any programming language.

Located in the diverse cultural hub of Montréal, Plotly is constantly looking for innovative, motivated, and passionate individuals to join our team. Our work culture engenders skill-sharing, work-life balance, and open discussion. Despite working hard to create leading-edge products, we always make sure to take care of our employees and celebrate our efforts through initiatives such as our full-stocked snack and beverage bar, as well as hosting catered team lunches every Friday. On top of ensuring that our team is well-fed, we offer perks such as an office located in the heart of Montreal's Mile End (surrounded by hip cafes, restaurants, etc - ask us where our favourite cookies are!), a beautiful open office space, access to tech community events that are hosted within our office space.
